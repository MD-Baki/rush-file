FAQ's

Payment – Do I have to store my credit card details on registration?

No. You can elect to store your credit card details with PayU’s secure payment gateway, but this is optionally for your convenience. This allows for quicker payment without having to re-enter your details. Rush will never request or store your credit card details.

Payment - How do I receive my refund?

Should a refund be due to you, we can reimburse you to the bank account (7th of the month), or credit card (up to 10 days to reflect) from which you paid for your order. We can also credit your wallet (within 24 hours), or add a voucher to your account (within 24 hours).

Registration - Why did I not receive my email for verification?

Please check in your junk mail or spam folder, make sure the email address you have entered is correct, or contact support for assistance.

Registration - Do you need my ID and Credit card number to register?

No, you are not required to provide this information. If paying by credit card, you will only enter this at checkout directly with the secure payment gateway.

Courier Services - Why is the Budget service more expensive than the Overnight service?

The minimum charge for the Budget service includes parcels up to 10kg, where other services minimum charge only includes 2kg of weight.

Courier Service - When do I use Budget?

For large or heavy parcels and for deliveries that are not urgent.
